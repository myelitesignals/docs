# Does EliteAlgo Repaint Its Signals?

EliteAlgo signals **do not repaint**. They are generated in **real-time** and confirmed only when the candle closes, ensuring accuracy and reliability.

### What is Repainting?

Repainting occurs when signals are adjusted based on future data, making them appear differently than how they originally were. With EliteAlgo, you can trust that what you see is what you get.
