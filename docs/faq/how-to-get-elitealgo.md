# How To Get EliteAlgo

## Follow These Steps for Access

### 1. Visit Our Website

Go to [elitesignals.com](https://elitesignals.com/#pricing).

### 2. Choose a Plan

Select the Starter plan or the Advanced plan. Both plans include EliteAlgo. The Advanced plan also includes premium access to ChartLabs, and the expanded EliteAlgo toolkit.

- Both plans are currently offered for just **$1 for the first 30 days**.
- You can also opt for **Yearly Access**: Full access for a year, starting anytime.
- You can also opt for **Lifetime Access**: Permanent access to EliteAlgo and the Elite Signals community, including all future updates, early feature previews, and beta testing opportunities.

### 3. Complete Your Signup

After signing up, you'll receive an **"Order Confirmation"** with instructions. Please check that it did not go to your spam folder before reaching out. You will also be redirected to this page: [https://elitesignals.com/tv-access](https://elitesignals.com/tv-access)

The access is automated. Please follow the instructions to get instant access.

### 4. Activation

This is instantly granted once our system verifies your order details (1-2 minutes).

### 5. Troubleshooting

If you provided an incorrect username or don't see access reflected after following the instructions, contact our Admin team via:

- **Email**: [support@elitesignals.com](mailto:support@elitesignals.com)
